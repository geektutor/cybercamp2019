Most Nigerian kids look forward to their long vacation from July – September of every year, it is usually a period to get away from the rigors of academics and to have lots of fun. However, this period can be optimized by ensuring that the young ones have fun while learning new skills.

Unilag Consult’s Cyber Camp 2019 for teenagers is the second edition of the programme, the initiative was born on the premise and need to capacitate young Nigerians with modern technological skill-sets in selected specializations.

The world as we know it today has evolved exponentially and continues to do so, especially in the area of Information Technology. A report from the United States Bureau of Statistics showed that Computer occupations made up nearly 45 percent of Science, Technology, and Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) employments in the United States in 2015. While it projects employment in computer occupations to increase by 12.5 percent from 2014 to 2024, and due to its large employment size, this growth is expected to result in nearly half a million new jobs, far more than any other STEM group.

We intend to adopt a project based (practical) delivery model at impacting the participants with hands-on skills.

Course Categories:

1. Web Development Unlock your potential and learn how to build a website, exploring the fundamentals of coding structures and principles, problem solving skills and logical thinking processes through interactive projects using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more.

2. Mobile Apps / Application Programming Build an original app for Android or iOS by learning the fundamentals of app development; programming, event handling, debugging, design and prototyping. Create simple interfaces, manage multiple views and learn to code in Swift, Python, Java, Scratch and more.

3. Digital Illustration Discover the design process as you explore 2D illustration and animation by drawing and illustrating your own animations. Learn to use Photoshop to get an in-depth look at the professional illustration pipeline, focusing on illustration and pre-production.

4. Robotics + Engineering Learn computer science concepts, from computational thinking through writing interactive programs, and see your robots in action. Hands-on robotics programming and engineering using Arduino. This growing field impacts the world, from medicine to farming.

5. Game Design and Development designing digital games requires decision-making, time and project management, presentation skills, and the ability to generate ideas. Learn professional techniques and tools used by industry pros to make hit video games.

Duration: The camp will last for 6 weeks from Monday July 22 to Friday September 6, 2019. Training sessions will hold from 9am to 2pm every day.

Certification: Participants will get a certificate of technical proficiency.
